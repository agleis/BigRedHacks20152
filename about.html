About

How many times have you been stranded in an unfamiliar public area, having no knowledge of the nearby restroom facilities? In cases such as these, we've often had to settle for mediocrity, or worse.

With Flushr©, those dark days are long behind you. Flushr keeps track of the public restrooms nearest you, and with the help of hundreds of users like yourself, every restroom will be associated with its own detailed ratings and feedback. We aim to provide the best data on public restrooms, all to help you make informed decisions.

To add your own review, just click on the '+' icon within the pin for a building. Be sure to let us know if you experienced any epiphanies, and add a comment if you want.

From humble beginnings at Big Red Hacks 2015, we at Flushr hope to first serve the Cornell community, then shift our focus to the entire world, empowering all humanity with the knowledge they deserve.

We hope you'll find our service as exciting as we do. We're just doing our small part in making the world a better place. Enjoy!

Sincerely, the Flushr team
